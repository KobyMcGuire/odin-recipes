Lasagna

Description

This recipe was passed down from generation to generation in my family. I feel it is now the time to release this godly lasagna to the public. This lasagna not only tastes like it was found in the Garden of Eden, but it will cure you of all your ailments.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Lay out pasta in lasagna fashion
  2. Place cooked meat and ricotta cheese on the laid out pasta
  3. Put the lasagna in the oven for twenty minutes
  4. Enjoy God's gift to man
