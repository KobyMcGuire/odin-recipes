Apple Pie

Description

Do you remember the feeling when your grandma told you that she was baking an apple pie? No? Well now you will know true jubilation. As soon as this apple pie hits your lips, you may just self combust beccause of how good it tastes.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Pie Crust
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Place pie crust down in pie dish
  2. Peel and slice apples
  3. Place apples in pie crust
  4. Bake pie at 450 degrees for 30 minutes
